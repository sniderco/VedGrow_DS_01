# COVID-19 Global Data Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs an exploratory and comparative analysis of COVID-19 data across multiple countries and WHO regions. The objective is to identify patterns in confirmed cases, recovery rates, death rates, and disease spread using data visualization and statistical analysis.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the global impact of COVID-19.
- Identify the most affected countries.
- Compare death and recovery rates across WHO regions.
- Study continent-wise spread and recovery patterns.
- Generate actionable insights using data visualization.

## Major Findings

- The United States reported the highest number of confirmed cases among analyzed countries.
- Brazil and India followed as the next most affected countries.
- Recovery rates varied significantly across countries and WHO regions.
- Some countries maintained high recovery rates despite elevated case counts.
- Death rates differed considerably across regions, highlighting healthcare and policy differences.
- Several countries in the Americas demonstrated recovery rates above 90%.
- Yemen exhibited the highest death rate within the Eastern Mediterranean region.
